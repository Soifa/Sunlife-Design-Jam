ğŸ‘¥ User Personas

We focused on two key personas to guide our user-centered design approach:

  • Maria: A 33-year-old software engineer who recently started her first job. She faces student debt, credit card challenges, and anxiety around financial management. She needs simple tools, friendly guidance, and a low-barrier way to start saving and learning.
  • Alex: A 24-year-old marketing intern just beginning his career. He is interested in investing but lacks foundational knowledge and feels overwhelmed by the complexity of available tools. He needs confidence-building support and beginner-friendly education.
